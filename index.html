    Why we're building this

    Most goal apps confuse activity with progress. They count check-ins, log streaks, and reward the act of opening the app. Time passes. The goal doesn't move.

    They reward showing up, not finishing. Habits are not goals. Streaks are not outcomes. A perfect 365-day streak with nothing completed is a beautifully decorated treadmill.

    Powerful Progress will track completion, not consistency. You'll set a goal with a deadline. Get the smallest action that moves it forward today. The math will take care of itself — you'll take care of the work.
